Filter Phil — the first email bot, live. I set up @Filter_Phil_bot under the AIOS project. He watches my Gmail around the clock and only pings me on Telegram when an email genuinely needs me — a real person, money, a deadline, a contract. ★
Voice Agents Project. Coming out of the meeting with Teemu, I scoped a personalized AI voice-agent system that calls Mike’s existing loyalty customers (roughly 102,000, with purchase history back to 2017) for customer-care and re-engagement — not cold calling. The plan: Retell AI for the voice agent plus Twilio for telephony, a Python layer that analyzes each call and writes the outcome back to the CRM, about $0.15 per active call minute (~$450 for a 1,000-customer pilot), a custom CRM on AIOS + Supabase as the long-term home, and a proper Canadian/Quebec compliance review plus a security audit before anything launches. The full proposal lands next week — built to be a key marketing engine for Little Tree first, then rolled out across the other companies.
